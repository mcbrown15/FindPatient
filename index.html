Legal Information

Federal Regulations require us to tell you about our referral practices.

Participants in the "Find a Physician Database" are all active members of the medical staffs at Strong Memorial and Highland Hospitals, in certain medical staff categories. For more information about the participating categories, see below. No fee is charged to the participating doctors or to consumers.

The Find a Physician database will display all physicians who have privileges in a certain specialty, or will display the name of a particular doctor, depending on how you search (by name or specialty). Individuals can be excluded from the database if they resign their medical staff privileges, if those privileges are not renewed, or if they convert to a medical staff category that is not included in the database.

The Find a Physician Database includes physicians with medical staff privileges at Strong Memorial Hospital and Highland Hospital with privileges in the following categories:

  • Attending with Admitting
  • Attending w/o Admitting
  • Refer and Follow
  • Courtesy
  • Courtsey with Admitting
  • Courtsey w/o Admitting
